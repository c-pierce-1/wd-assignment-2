Top 3 Tate Street Food Recommendations

By Charles Pierce

Hungry? You wouldn't be reading this blog otherwise! You've come to the right place, as I will give you my personal best recommendations for restaurants on tate street! So strap in, as we go through this mouth watering blog!

Sushi Republic

Address: 329 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403
Phone: 336-274-6684
Hours: M-F 11:30AM-2:30PM, 5-9:30PM.
SAT 5-10PM

Spider Jr Roll (Credit: Sushi Republic, Google Reviews)

Deep fried soft shell crab, avocado, cucumber, eel sauce

Sweet Potato Tempura (Credit: Sushi Republic, Google Reviews)

Sweet potato tempura, cucumber, avocado

Salmon Avocado Sashimi (Credit: Sushi Republic, Google Reviews)

Salmon poke wrapped in avocadowith ponzu sauce

New York Pizza

Address: 337 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403
Phone: 336-763-1426
Hours: M-F 11AM-2PM
SAT 12PM-2AM
SUN 1PM-12AM

Stromboli (Credit: New York Pizza, Google Reviews)

Pepperoni, sausage, mozzarella, sauce.

Buffalo Chicken Pizza (Credit: New York Pizza, Google Reviews)

Buffalo sauce, grilled chicken and mozzarella cheese.

Steak and Cheese (Credit: New York Pizza, Google Reviews)

Steak, american cheese, lettuce, tomato & mayo.

Crazy Mexico Restaurant

Address: 332 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403
Phone: 336-500-8890
Hours: M-TH 11AM-10PM
FRI 10:30AM-10PM
SAT 12-10PM

Fajitas (Credit: crazymexicotate.com)

Tender beef or chicken fajitas with bell peppers, onions and tomatoes. Served with guacamole, sour cream, rice, beans and tortillas

Carne Asada (Credit: crazymexicotate.com)

Rib eye steak with grilled shrimp. Served with beans, lettuce, sliced avocado, pico de gallo and tortillas

Burrito Hawaiano (Credit: crazymexicotate.com)

Burrito filled with grilled chicken, steak, shrimp, pineapple, onions, peppers and tomatoes. Topped with bacon and cheese dip

Aside:

New York pizza is my personal favorite, but it definitely gets pricey! Pro tip: spending $60 or more gets you 10% off!
